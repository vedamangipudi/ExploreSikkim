Adventures

The thrill that Sikkim offers is beyond comparison; marked with 28 major mountain peaks, 80+ glaciers, 227 high-altitude lakes, five major hot springs, 100+ rivers and streams, this Northeastern State has enough challenges to quench the thirst of any adventure lover who loves trekking, mountaineering, river rafting, mountain biking or is willing to try something new and exciting.

Most Popular Adventure Activities in Sikkim:
-Trekking & Hiking
-River Rafting
-Mountaineering
-Mountain Biking
-Skiing
-Paragliding
-Hand Gliding
